FCR Metrics Explained
What is FCR?

Feed Conversion Ratio (FCR) is a measure of how efficiently your birds convert feed into body weight.

FCR Formula: Total Feed Consumed (kg) ÷ Total Weight Gain (kg)

Lower FCR is better - it means your birds are more efficient at converting feed to weight.

How Payments Are Calculated
  • Base payment: ₹10 per bird
  • FCR Bonus: 50% of feed cost saved when FCR is below target
  • Mortality Penalty: ₹5 per bird for mortality above 5%
