V.A. ChickVac

Day Administered: Day 7

V.A. ChickVac™ is a modified-live virus vaccine for the immunization of chickens against viral arthritis.

Storage:

  • Prolonged exposure to higher temperatures and/or direct sunlight may affect potency.
  • Do not freeze.
  • Store diluent at or below 80°F (27°C).

Precautions:

  • Rehydrate with Zoetis diluent only.
  • Vaccinate only healthy birds.
  • Do not vaccinate birds in egg production.
  • Use vaccine within one hour after rehydrating.

Administration:

  • This vaccine can be administered by Intraocular instillation or via drinking water..
